What is DiaspoCare?

DiaspoCare is an online tool that connects you directly to a network of qualified medical service providers abroad. Through DiaspoCare you can now manage, track, and pay for your loved one's medical needs from a distance using your desktop or any mobile device.

The DiaspoCare team is comprised of members of the Diaspora who started the company to address their own frustrations with the inconsitent quality of healthcare and the abundance of counterfeit medicines encountered in their home countries abroad.

Why DiaspoCare?

We rigorously screen and partner with the best doctors and licensed pharmacists to uncover and treat health issues that threaten the well-being of your loved ones.

We protect patient's privacy and your confidential information as well as ensure the integrity of every transaction by adhering to stringent standards.

How DiaspoCare works

Arrange for services

Using any computer or mobile device, signup and enroll as many friends and family members as you want. Once a person is enrolled easily arrange for medical services from a network of qualified professionals.

Stay up to date

Receive notifications for important health events such as completed doctor's visits and prescription refills.

Pay directly

Remove the need to wire cash by securely and directly paying medical providers through the online portal. You only pay for services actually delivered.

DiaspoCare beta is currently live in Lagos, Nigeria.
